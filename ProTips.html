How To Select A Collage

  • First select some collages.

  • Then enquire about the collages from the Alumni or student who is studing there.

  • Then shortlist the collages based on the distance.

  • Then visit the collages first.

  • Don't pay the money to confirm the seat unless you have low cutoff.

  • Then check whether you can get seat via counselling by comparing your cutoff with the previous year counselling result.

  • Money plays an important role while joining collages.

  • Even if you get low marks if you have money you can join top collages.
